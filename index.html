About Us

We invite you to WEST Restaurant & Bar to embark on a culinary journey that transcends the ordinary.
Nestled in the heart of Dublin, our haven for food enthusiasts is more than a dining destination; it's a celebration of flavors, creativity, and unforgettable moments.

At WEST Restaurant & Bar, we stand as a testament to Dublin's rich culinary heritage and a beacon of gastronomic excellence. Our story begins decades ago when our founders, inspired by the city's vibrant cultural tapestry, envisioned a dining experience that would transcend time. Originally established in 1979, WEST has witnessed the evolution of Dublin's culinary landscape, adapting and innovating while staying true to our roots.

Join us in this culinary journey, where history and innovation converge, creating an experience that transcends time and tantalizes taste buds.
